Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Who we are

AllWins (website) is a platform that helps businesses display and analyze social metrics. Contact us at privacy@allwins.ai.

What data we collect

  • Website data: basic server logs and performance telemetry.
  • Instagram (Meta) data: upon your consent via OAuth, we access read-only fields such as: account id, username, account type, follower count; and media metadata (id, caption, media type/url, permalink, like & comment counts, timestamp). Certain insights may be retrieved where permitted by your business/creator account.
  • Pinterest data: upon your consent via OAuth, we access read-only fields such as: account profile, boards, and pins metadata (images/links/descriptions) and, where permitted, analytics.

How we use the data

  • To display your own authorized metrics in your dashboard or public profile (only when you choose to publish).
  • To provide support and improve the service.
  • We do not sell your data. We do not post on your behalf.

Legal basis & consent

We process social account data only after you explicitly authorize our app via each platform’s OAuth screen. You can revoke access at any time using platform settings (Meta Account Center, Pinterest settings) or by contacting us.

Data sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties except service providers necessary to operate the site (e.g., hosting) under data processing agreements. If legally required, we may disclose information to comply with law.

Data retention

We store tokens and fetched metrics only as long as needed for the features you enable. You may request deletion of your data and tokens at any time by emailing privacy@allwins.ai.

Security

We use HTTPS, access controls, and industry-standard practices to protect data. Sensitive credentials are kept server-side and never in client code.

Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object or restrict processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Children

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

International transfers

Data may be processed in countries other than your own. Where applicable, we use appropriate safeguards.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will update the “Last updated” date and, if material, notify you within the product.
